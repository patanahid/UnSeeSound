What is UnSeeSound and how to use it?

UnSeeSound is a helpful utility tool designed to assist users when writing or copying text from devices such as mobiles and desktops. It eliminates the need to constantly switch focus between the device's screen and a separate notebook. UnSeeSound converts your input into spoken words, making it easier for you to transcribe or review your text.

Using UnSeeSound is incredibly simple. Just follow these steps:


1. Type your desired text in the input box provided.
2. Click the "Star t" button to initiate the text-to-speech conversion.
3. The first word will be spoken immediately after you enter it in the input box.
4. To speak the next word, simply press the Spacebar on your keyboard. On mobile devices, tap the "Next Word" button.
5. If you're having trouble understanding the last spoken word, you can click the keys above the Spacebar ("z," "x," "c," "v," "b," "n," "m") to get the spelling of the last word.
6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you have completed your text.
